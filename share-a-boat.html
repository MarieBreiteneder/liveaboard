Guide for soon-to-be Boat owners

how to start - do i need to buy?

the answer is no. there are many Boat owners out there who are looking for live a Board crew all over the world.
use our crew finder and to find a bunk for your adventure or to find crew members for your home.
some Boat owners hire crew for Boat deliveries or as liveaBoard technicians
or just to look after their Boat while they are away on land.
